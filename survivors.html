Survivor Testimony

In Susanne Riegler’s 18-minute documentary from 2021, the focus is on the concept of “work” in the context of prostitution. Four women who managed to leave the red-light scene behind, expose what the mainstream discourse conceals as: “work”.

The reality is: around the clock availability, without legal protection, exposed to numerous forms of violence.

By the end of the film, one cannot help but ask how anyone could ever come up with the idea of considering prostitution a “job option” and a “service sector.”
