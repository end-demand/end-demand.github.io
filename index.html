About End Demand Switzerland

End Demand Switzerland (EDS) is a non-profit organization promoting strategies that focus on the root causes of human trafficking for the purpose of sexual exploitation and prostitution. In particular, the demand for commercial sex. Without this demand there would be no trafficking for sexual exploitation.
